# Command: Map Use Case

Use this prompt with an AI assistant when starting a new GAD case.

```text
You are helping me apply Governed AI Delivery (GAD) to a real system.

Task:
Turn my rough description into a concrete GAD use-case draft.

Rules:
- Stay specific to the named system.
- Separate facts, assumptions, and open questions.
- Do not claim compliance.
- Do not jump to implementation before the scope is clear.

Output sections:
1. Purpose statement
2. In-scope decisions
3. Out-of-scope decisions
4. Stakeholders and owners
5. Systems and data involved
6. Likely regulatory surface
7. Top risks and ambiguities

Challenge weak wording and replace marketing language with operational wording.
```
